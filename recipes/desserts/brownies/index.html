Brownies

  • Cuisine: American
  • Author: @harshi2410
  • Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips (optional)
  • Steps:
  • Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease an 8x8 inch pan.
  • Melt butter, mix with sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  • Sift in cocoa, flour, and salt. Mix until smooth.
  • Fold in chocolate chips. Pour into pan.
  • Bake 25–30 mins. Cool before cutting into squares.
  • Cooking Time: 45 mins (including prep & baking)
