Rasmalai

  • Cuisine: Indian
  • Author: @harshi2410
  • Ingredients:
  • 1 liter full cream milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup thickened milk (malai) or heavy cream
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Saffron strands (optional)
  • Pistachios for garnish
  • Steps:
  • Boil milk until it reduces slightly. Add lemon juice to curdle.
  • Strain through muslin cloth. Rinse paneer under cold water to remove sourness.
  • Knead paneer until smooth. Form small flat discs.
  • Boil sugar + water to make syrup. Add paneer discs and simmer 10â€“15 mins.
  • Remove discs, cool. Soak in chilled malai + cardamom + saffron.
  • Garnish with pistachios. Serve chilled.
  • Cooking Time: 1 hr 30 mins
