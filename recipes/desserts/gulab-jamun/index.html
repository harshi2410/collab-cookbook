Gulab Jamun

  • Cuisine: Indian
  • Author: @harshi2410
  • Ingredients:
  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1–2 tbsp milk (to bind)
  • Oil for frying
  • For sugar syrup:
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 1 cup water
    • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
    • 1–2 drops rose water (optional)
  • Steps:
  • Mix milk powder, flour, cardamom. Add milk gradually to form a soft dough.
  • Shape into small balls (no cracks).
  • Heat oil on low-medium flame. Fry balls until deep golden brown.
  • Meanwhile, boil sugar + water + cardamom + rose water for 5–7 mins to make syrup.
  • Soak fried balls in hot syrup for 15–20 mins. Serve warm or chilled.
  • Cooking Time: 45 mins
