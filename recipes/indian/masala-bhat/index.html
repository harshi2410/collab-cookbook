Masala Bhat (Spiced Rice)

  • Cuisine: Indian
  • Author: @harshi2410
  • Ingredients:
  • 1 cup rice (cooked)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp sambar powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish
  • Steps:
  • Heat oil, add mustard seeds and urad dal. Let them splutter.
  • Add onion, green chili, and sauté till golden.
  • Add tomato, turmeric, sambar powder, salt. Cook until mushy.
  • Add cooked rice and mix gently. Cook for 2–3 mins.
  • Garnish with coriander. Serve hot with papad or pickle.
  • Cooking Time: 20 mins
