Paneer Tikka

  • Cuisine: Indian
  • Author: @harshi2410
  • Ingredients:
  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil (for grilling)
  • Steps:
  • In a bowl, mix yogurt, garam masala, turmeric, lemon juice, and salt.
  • Add paneer cubes and marinate for 30 minutes.
  • Skewer the paneer (optional) and grill or bake at 200°C for 15–20 minutes until golden.
  • Serve hot with mint chutney.
  • Cooking Time: 45 mins (including marination)
